Indian Classical Music Instruments

String Instruments

Sitar: A plucked string instrument used in Hindustani classical music.

Veena: A traditional Indian string instrument used in Carnatic music.

Percussion Instruments

Tabla: A popular Indian percussion instrument used in Hindustani music.

Mridangam: A primary rhythmic instrument in Carnatic music.

Wind Instruments

Bansuri: A bamboo flute associated with Indian classical music.

Shehnai: A double-reed wind instrument commonly used in North Indian music.
